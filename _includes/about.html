About

Having 34 years of experience in the field of Design, Development, and Realization of high precision space-qualified components at ISRO.

Played a key role in the manufacturing of mission-critical precision components with close tolerances and critical assemblies through its precision machining, assembly, specialized fabrication, testing, and quality control processes.

Closely interacted with parties having state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities in the country which can undertake precision machining, assembly, specialized fabrication, heat treatment, testing, quality control, and other specialized processes.

Worked as a team lead for development and fabrication of molds of antenna reflectors of Spacecraft. Worked in the areas for use of advance materials in the field of Space Applications . Worked as a team lead for vendor evaluation and development for fabrication of highly precision components.

Published 9 papers in various National & International Conferences. Successfully developed mechanical parts in CFRP and other advanced composites for Space use. Highly complex components were developed by Additive Manufacturing Techniques.
